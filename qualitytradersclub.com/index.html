Frequently Asked Question

 

 
What if I know nothing about trading?

If you don't have experience with trading, don't worry. Our program is designed to accommodate beginners. We start with the basics of forex trading, so you understand the fundamental concepts before we move on to more advanced strategies.

Our community and resources are arranged to help beginners develop their skills at their own pace. With our structured learning method, hands-on practice, and supportive environment, you will gain the knowledge and confidence necessary to trade effectively, even if you're starting from scratch.

How much does it take to get started?

To start on our forex trading course, is completely free.

Besides this modest amount, the most important thing you need to bring is a commitment and a willingness to learn.

Regardless of your previous experience or background, you are welcome to join and start building your knowledge and skills in forex trading. We provide the necessary resources and support so you can develop and grow as a trader.

When will I see results?

It’s important to understand that results in forex trading can vary from person to person, and they depend on many different factors, including your learning curve, how much time you invest in trading and learning, as well as market conditions.

Some may start to see results within a few hours, while for others it may take longer.

We focus on providing you with the necessary tools and knowledge so you can develop effective trading strategies. Remember, trading also involves risk, and it’s important to approach it with realistic expectations and patience.

We recommend continuous learning and practical application of your skills to improve your chances of success in the long term.
